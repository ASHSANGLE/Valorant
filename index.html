Welcome to Valorant Fan Page

Valorant is a free-to-play first-person tactical shooter by Riot Games. Released in 2020, the game has captured the attention of millions of players worldwide.

About Valorant

Valorant combines tactical gunplay with unique agent abilities. It requires a combination of strategy, teamwork, and precise aiming to succeed.

Each agent has distinct abilities that define their playstyle. With a vast arsenal of weapons and tactical options, every match feels fresh and competitive.

Gameplay Modes

Valorant offers a variety of exciting game modes to enjoy:

  • Unrated: The standard 5v5 mode where teams compete to plant or defuse the spike.
  • Competitive: Like Unrated, but with a ranked system that tests your skill level.
  • Spike Rush: A faster-paced, shorter match format with random weapon loadouts.
  • Deathmatch: Pure combat to practice your aim without the pressure of objectives.
