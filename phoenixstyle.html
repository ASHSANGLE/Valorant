Phoenix - Duelist

Phoenix is a fire-based duelist with abilities that can heal him or deal damage to enemies. He excels in close-range combat.

Abilities

  • Hot Hands: Throws a fireball that explodes after a delay or impact.
  • Blaze: Creates a wall of fire that blocks vision and damages anyone passing through it.
  • Curveball: Throws a flashbang that curves around corners.
  • Run it Back: Phoenix can respawn at a marked location if killed during the ability's duration.
