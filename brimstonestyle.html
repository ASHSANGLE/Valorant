Brimstone - Controller

Brimstone is a master of area control, capable of launching powerful airstrikes and creating smokescreens to block enemy vision.

Abilities

  • Sky Smoke: Deploys a smoke screen to block vision.
  • Stim Beacon: Increases fire rate for teammates within range.
  • Incendiary: Fires a grenade that leaves a burning area.
  • Orbital Strike: Calls in a devastating airstrike over a selected area.
